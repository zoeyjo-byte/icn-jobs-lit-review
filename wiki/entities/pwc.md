## PwC

PwC is a global professional services firm that published the [[2026-global-ai-jobs-barometer-full-report]], detailing the impact of AI on the job market.

### Key Findings
- PwC's report highlights the **democratization** and **professionalization** of jobs due to AI.
- The report also discusses the **wage premiums** for AI skills.
