## Update 2026-06-26

Figure 15 in the PwC 2026 Global AI Jobs Barometer shows wage premiums for AI skills have risen to 62% on average across sectors (up from 57% in 2025), with significant variation: Consumer Markets leads at 118%, followed by Technology, Media and Telecoms (84%), Energy (75%), and Manufacturing (73%). Government shows the lowest premium (16%). Figure 16 reveals AI specialist hiring has grown eight times faster than total job postings since 2012, with Technology, Media and Telecoms accounting for 11.4% of these roles globally by 2025. This surge correlates with the 33.5% productivity growth in highly AI-exposed companies, where AI skills increasingly intersect with domain expertise in high-growth sectors.
