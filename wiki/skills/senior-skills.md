Senior skills encompass competencies traditionally required in more senior positions that are now increasingly appearing in entry-level job descriptions due to AI adoption. These include strategic oversight, complex problem-solving, cross-functional leadership, and domain expertise application. As AI handles routine execution, junior roles absorb responsibilities that previously required 3-5 years of experience, compressing career progression timelines. This trend creates 'seniorised' entry positions that demand immediate high-value contribution, raising barriers to labor market entry while offering accelerated development pathways for those who qualify.
