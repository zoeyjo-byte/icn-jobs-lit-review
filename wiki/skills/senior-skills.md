## Update 2026-06-26

The PwC 2026 Global AI Jobs Barometer (Figure 8) provides concrete evidence of the "seniorisation" trend in entry-level roles. In the US, entry-level job postings requiring traditionally senior skills are projected to grow by 35% between 2019 and 2025, while non-seniorised roles decline by 10%. This acceleration is concentrated in the top AI exposure quartile, where AI adoption drives demand for strategic thinking and complex problem-solving even at junior levels. The report attributes this to AI handling routine tasks, pushing human workers toward activities like negotiating contracts (previously reserved for senior recruiters) and managing stakeholder relationships—skills now appearing in 22% of professionalised job descriptions.
