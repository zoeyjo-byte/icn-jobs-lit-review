PwC's proprietary methodology to measure the degree to which different occupations are exposed to AI capabilities. The index analyzes occupational task composition against current AI capabilities to categorize jobs into exposure quartiles, enabling longitudinal tracking of AI's labor market impact. It serves as the foundation for identifying democratized versus professionalized job pathways and analyzing differential effects across sectors. The methodology combines technical assessment of AI capabilities with labor market data to provide actionable insights for workforce strategy and policy development.
