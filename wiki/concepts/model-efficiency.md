# Model Efficiency

**Type:** Concept
**First observed:** 2026-05-27
**Last updated:** 2026-05-27

## Definition
The trend toward smaller, cheaper models that achieve competitive performance — a market response to the token crunch and rising inference costs.

## Key examples
- Cursor Composer 2.5: Third on Coding Agent Index at 10-60x lower cost than leaders.
- Google Gemma 4: Surging adoption as cheap alternative to Chinese models.
