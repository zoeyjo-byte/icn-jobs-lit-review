# Token Crunch

**Type:** Concept
**First observed:** 2026-05-27
**Last updated:** 2026-05-27

## Definition
The end of the AI subsidy era — companies exhausting token budgets as free or heavily subsidized experimentation gives way to sustainable pricing. Characterized by organizations burning through annual AI budgets in months and rationing access.

## Key examples
- Uber burned through full-year 2026 AI budget in four months.
- US government rationing access to most powerful models.
- [[tokenmaxxing]] emerging as a productivity hack.
- Supply growing 3x/year vs. demand at 10x/year (Epoch AI).
