# AI Slowdown Panic

**Type:** Concept
**First observed:** 2026-05-27 (pattern observed since 2023)
**Last updated:** 2026-05-27

## Definition
A recurring annual phenomenon (typically summer) where narratives of an AI bubble or slowdown gain traction. Each year takes a different shape: 2023 (ChatGPT's first down month), 2024 (pre-training wall), 2025 (MIT study claiming 95% AI project failure, GPT-5 underwhelming), 2026 (token budgets running out, end of subsidy era).

## Key examples
- 2026 version: Token budgets exhausted, inference costs rising, professional critics weaving bubble narrative.
- Counter-narrative: Demand still outrunning supply 10x vs 3x, GPU prices up 2x, market maturing rather than popping.
