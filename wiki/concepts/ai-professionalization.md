## AI Professionalization

AI professionalization refers to the process where AI automates less expert tasks, leaving more expert tasks for humans. This trend is observed in 22% of jobs, as per the [[2026-global-ai-jobs-barometer-full-report]].

### Key Findings
- **22% of jobs** are being professionalized.
- Professionalized roles demand additional skills at **twice the rate** of democratized roles.
