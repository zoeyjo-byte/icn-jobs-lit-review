Seniorised roles describe the phenomenon where entry-level positions increasingly demand skills traditionally required in senior positions due to AI adoption. As AI handles routine tasks, junior roles absorb more complex responsibilities that previously required years of experience. This compresses career progression timelines and raises barriers to labor market entry, as new graduates must demonstrate capabilities once associated with mid-career professionals. The trend is particularly evident in technology-intensive fields where AI reshapes job architectures, creating 'on-ramp' positions that blend entry-level accessibility with senior-level complexity.
