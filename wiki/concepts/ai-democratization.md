## AI Democratization

AI democratization refers to the process where AI automates more expert tasks, leaving less expert tasks for humans. This trend is observed in 52% of jobs, as per the [[2026-global-ai-jobs-barometer-full-report]].

### Key Findings
- **52% of jobs** are being democratized.
- **22% of jobs** are being professionalized.
