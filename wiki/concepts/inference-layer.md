# Inference Layer

**Type:** Concept
**First observed:** 2026-05-27
**Last updated:** 2026-05-27

## Definition
The infrastructure and market for serving AI model inference (as opposed to training). Represents a shift in the AI industry value chain from building models to running them at scale.

## Key examples
- Baseten raising $1B at $11B valuation, revenue tripling to $600M annualized.
- OpenRouter becoming a unicorn at $1.3B, processing 100T tokens/month.
- Epoch AI data: global inference capacity tripling yearly, demand growing 10x.
