# AI Jobs Apocalypse

**Type:** Concept
**First observed:** 2026-05-27
**Last updated:** 2026-05-27

## Definition
The narrative that AI will cause massive job displacement, particularly in white-collar roles. In 2026, AI leaders began walking back this prediction.

## Key examples
- Sam Altman: No longer believes in jobs apocalypse; admits intuitions about entry-level displacement were off.
- David Solomon (Goldman Sachs): AI displaced 16% of entry-level tasks but will create more jobs than it destroys.
