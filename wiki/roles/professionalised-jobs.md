## Update 2026-06-26

The PwC 2026 Global AI Jobs Barometer (Figure 4) shows professionalised roles (22% of global jobs) demand additional skills at twice the rate of democratised roles, with 68% growth in skills required since 2018 versus 33%. This gap widened significantly after 2022 as AI automated routine tasks like CV screening, pushing humans toward complex activities like contract negotiation. Figure 5 confirms these roles grew 39% in postings (vs 17% for democratised jobs) and saw 37% salary growth since 2021 (vs 26%), representing 42% faster compensation increases. Figure 9 reveals their skills are changing 2.2x faster than least-exposed jobs, with the gap accelerating annually—reaching 116% more change by 2025.
