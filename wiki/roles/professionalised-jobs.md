Professionalised jobs are occupations reshaped by AI to demand more human expertise, representing 22% of advertised positions globally. In these roles, AI automates less expert tasks (e.g., routine data processing), leaving more complex, expert-level work for humans (e.g., strategic interpretation). This pathway elevates the human role, creating high-growth, high-wage opportunities that require advanced domain knowledge and critical thinking. Professionalised roles are prevalent in fields like healthcare, engineering, and strategic consulting, where AI serves as a productivity enhancer rather than a replacement for human judgment.
