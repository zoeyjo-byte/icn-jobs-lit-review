## Update 2026-06-26

As detailed in Figure 2 of the PwC 2026 Global AI Jobs Barometer, democratised jobs (52% of global positions) involve AI automating more expert tasks—like inventory management for clerks or contract negotiation for recruiters—leaving less expert work for humans. Figure 4 shows these roles have seen 33% growth in skills demanded since 2018 (half the rate of professionalised jobs), while Figure 5 indicates 17% job growth and 26% salary growth since 2021. Despite AI handling expert tasks, Figure 11 reveals democratised jobs have the highest EPOCH score (0.48), meaning they still critically depend on human capabilities. Figure 10 confirms new tasks in these roles are 2.5x more likely to require empathy and face-to-face presence than in low-exposure jobs.
