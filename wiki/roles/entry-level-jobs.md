## Update 2026-06-26

According to Figure 7 in the PwC 2026 Global AI Jobs Barometer, entry-level job postings in the highest AI exposure quartile have flatlined since 2022 (coinciding with ChatGPT's release), while lower-exposure quartiles continue growing—reaching 8.0x 2012 levels by 2025 for the bottom 25%. Figure 8 further breaks down the US top-quartile roles: "seniorised" entry-level positions (requiring traditionally senior skills) are projected to grow 35% between 2019-2025, while non-seniorised roles decline 10%. This bifurcation reflects AI's dual impact—automating routine tasks in high-exposure roles while creating demand for human-intensive capabilities in client management and complex decision-making.
