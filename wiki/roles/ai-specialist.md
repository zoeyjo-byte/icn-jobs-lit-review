## AI Specialist

AI specialists are in high demand, with job postings growing **eight times faster** than total job postings, as per the [[2026-global-ai-jobs-barometer-full-report]].

### Key Findings
- AI specialist job postings grew from **0.0** in 2012 to **11.0** in 2025.
- **Technology, Media and Telecoms** lead in AI specialist hiring.
