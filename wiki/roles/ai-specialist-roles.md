## Update 2026-06-26

Figure 16 in the PwC 2026 Global AI Jobs Barometer shows AI specialist job postings have grown eight times faster than total job postings from 2012-2025. Relative to 2012, AI specialist roles reached 11.0 by 2025 while total jobs only grew to 2.0. The report breaks down sector adoption: Technology, Media and Telecoms leads with 11.4% of global AI specialist roles by 2025, followed by Health Industries (4.0%) and Financial Services (3.0%). This surge correlates with the 62% average wage premium for AI skills and explains the 33.5% productivity growth in highly AI-exposed companies. These roles increasingly require hybrid expertise—combining technical AI skills with domain knowledge in high-growth sectors like Consumer Markets (118% wage premium).
